ENROLL NOW! HERE ARE THE REQUIREMENTS

FOR FRESHMEN:

1. The original and three (3) photocopies of your Grade 12/Senior High School card (F138) duly signed by the head of your school. A rubber-stamped facsimile should be countersigned by the principal or his/her authorized representative

2. The original and three (3) photocopies of your Certificate of Live Birth (PSA Certified)

3. Good Moral Certificate

4. Two (2) recently colored 2” x 2” pictures with printed name tag and signature of the student at the back.

FOR TRANSFEREE:

1. Transcript of Records (TOR)

2. The original and three (3) photocopies of Honorable Dismissal/Transfer Credentials

3. The original and three (3) photocopies of your Certificate of Live Birth (PSA Certified)

4. Good Moral Certificate

5. Two (2) recently colored 2” x 2” pictures with the printed name tag and signature of the student at the back.

FOR 2ND-4TH YEAR:

1. OVRF file in .pdf format.

2. Summary of Grades (SOG) also in .pdf format.
